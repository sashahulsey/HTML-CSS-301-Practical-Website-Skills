Along with delicious drinks, food and great prices, the Campus Club Bar hosts a variety of events throughout the year including Special Happy Hours, Pairing Dinners, Classes and Tastings.

About the Bar

The Campus Club Bar is the only bar on the University of Minnesota-Twin Cities Campus, and we have been around for over 100 years.

The Campus Club Bar offers many local beers and spirits, along with a unique and affordale wine by the glass menu. Our chefs focus the food menus on locally sourced ingredients and seasonally changing menus.

We offer events throughout the year inclduing special summer happy hour events with local distilleries; beer, wine and cocktail pairing dinners; and a variety of classes and tastings.

Summer Happy Hours

The Campus Club Bar is open to non-members throughout the summer for our Happy Hour! Grab a beer and enjoy some of our happy hour appetizers while kicking back on the Terrace overlooking the Minneapolis skyline.

Pairing Dinners

The Bar offers themed beer, wine and cocktail pairing dinners throughout the year to members and non-members. Three food courses expertly paired with drinks for only $35!

Classes and Tastings

One perk of membership is the classes and tastings of wine, beer and spirits presented by top Twin Cities instructors. These classes offer the perfect opportunity to learn more about your favorite drinks and to discover new things!
